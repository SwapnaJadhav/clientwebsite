ABOUT US

Digivito is the brainchild of passionate, tech savvy professionals Pawan Yadav, Arti Agrawal and R. A. Yadav

Who have more than six decades of industrials experience collectively while working for giants like

IBM, Apple, Hero Honda and other large MNCs.

They bring extensive international experience in product development, services and consulting

across industries. They have built teams and delivered solutions using technologies such as

Automation-RPA, chatbots, AI/ML, Mobile and Web Applications and SAP Functional etc.

At Digivito, We approach each of our clients with fresh eyes to develop

customized and unique strategies/solutions.
